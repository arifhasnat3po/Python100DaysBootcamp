The Best Movies According to Arif

My top movies of all-time.

Interstellar

Plot: Earth is in a dystopian future ravaged by blight and famine. Former NASA pilot Cooper, now a farmer, is recruited for a desperate mission: travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for humanity.

In to the wild

"Into the Wild" can be a captivating non-fiction book about a young man's Alaskan odyssey, a powerful film that mirrors his journey, or a symbolic call to venture into the unknown and forge your own path, all wrapped in themes of freedom, self-discovery, and the raw power of nature. What sparks your curiosity?

Before Sunrise

"Before Sunrise" is a captivating 1995 romantic drama where two strangers, Jesse and Celine, meet on a train and spend a magical, whirlwind night exploring Vienna together. Their conversations, fueled by spontaneity and intellectual curiosity, delve into life, love, and the fleeting nature of connection. The film, filmed in real-time, captures the beauty of chance encounters and the intoxicating intimacy of shared moments under a foreign sky. Ethan Hawke and Julie Delpy deliver charming and nuanced performances, drawing you into their characters' growing connection as the sun rises on a night they'll never forget. Whether you're a hopeless romantic or simply appreciate a well-crafted story about human connection, "Before Sunrise" is a timeless classic that will leave you pondering the possibilities of love sparked by a single night.
